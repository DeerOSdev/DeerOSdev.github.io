ABOUT

Welcome to BirdOS! BirdOS is a fully debloated version of Windows 10. We've taken Microsoft's Windows 10 and removed all the unnecessary bloatware and clutter. The result is a faster, cleaner, and more efficient operating system.

Why BirdOS?

Speed and Performance: Windows 10 often comes with many pre-installed apps and features that slow down your computer. With BirdOS, we've removed all that extra stuff. This means your computer runs faster and smoother.

More Control: Microsoft's Windows 10 includes many features and apps you may not need or want. BirdOS gives you more control over your system. You decide what you want on your computer.

Better Privacy: We value your privacy. BirdOS reduces unnecessary data collection and tracking, giving you a more private computing experience.

Completely Clean: No pre-installed apps. BirdOS comes with a clean slate, allowing you to install only what you need.

Easy Customization: After connecting to the internet, you can do anything with the WinUtil icon on the desktop. This tool makes it simple to install and customize your applications and settings.

What We Fixed

Removed Bloatware: We've stripped out all the pre-installed apps and programs that slow down your system.

Improved Performance: By cutting out unnecessary features, BirdOS uses less memory and CPU, resulting in faster boot times and better overall performance.

Enhanced Privacy: BirdOS reduces the amount of data Microsoft collects from your system, offering you better privacy and security.

Simplified User Experience: We've streamlined the interface, making it easier and more intuitive to use.

Conclusion

BirdOS is the Windows 10 experience you deserve—fast, efficient, and in your control. Join us and enjoy a better, cleaner operating system with BirdOS!

ISO

Follow these simple steps to install BirdOS:

  1. Click the button below to download the BirdOS ISO file.
  2. Once the download is complete, use a tool like Rufus to create a bootable USB drive.
  3. Insert the bootable USB drive into the computer where you want to install BirdOS.
  4. Restart the computer and boot from the USB drive.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation of BirdOS.
